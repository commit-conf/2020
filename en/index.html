You do not live the same in video

Signing up the list of recorded talks, and "next weekend, I see them," or "one talk a day, during lunch." Totally. As soon as you take time.

Commit is not just an event of 140 talks. It is about meeting new people with similar interests; it is talking to the speaker after the talk; it is finding out what are looking for the leading companies in the country. If you stay at home, you miss the possibility of creating your own memories. It is not just about attending an event; It is about living the experience.

Commit is a conference that showcases all aspects of IT development with Spanish speakers, from software development techniques to Big Data, Cloud, or ML. It will happen at Universidad San Pablo CEU in Madrid on November 27-28.

Our commitment to diversity

Work does not discriminate. Work does not care about your gender, your religion, or who you share your life with. The only thing that matters is that it be done. And let it be done well. People do the rest.

Diversity in technological events leaves enough room for improvement. For this reason, we have decided to support different initiatives to help correct this.

Tech SHEssions

Tech SHEssions is a free program that we started three years ago to help technical women to leave the impostor syndrome behind and participate more actively in the communities, supported by mentors who have already traveled that same path.

» Read more about Tech SHEssions

Diversity tickets

Diversity is not just a matter of gender. If you are a member of an underrepresented group (including, but not limited to: non-Caucasians, LGBTQIA+, women, and people with disabilities), you can request one of our tickets available for diversity.

Registration for these tickets is not yet open, but we will announce it as soon as possible here and in our Twitter account.
