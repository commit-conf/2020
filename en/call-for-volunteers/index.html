Call for volunteers

The organization of Commit itself is a small team, and on the day of the event we need to be significantly more to face the challenges of a conference of this size:

  • Help in the rooms before, during and after the talks/labs.

  • Condition the common areas for the days of the event.

  • Preparation and delivery of swag to attendees.

  • Support the attendees looking for the tracks.

  • Help the speakers.

  • And whatever comes.

It's not all about duties, but advantages too:

  • Networking. Commit is a great opportunity to know a lot of awesome people.

  • Acquire first-hand experience about organizing an event of more than a thousand people.

  • Get swag and tshirt exclusive to Commit organizers.

  • You can attend some talks, with moderation. Remember, you came to contribute.


The registration opens on October 18th.
